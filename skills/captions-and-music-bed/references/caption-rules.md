# Caption Rules Reference

## Timing

- Captions should appear at the first spoken word of the caption chunk.
- Do not wait until the sentence is several words in.
- End the caption when the phrase completes or just before the next caption.
- For rapid speech, split into shorter chunks instead of allowing two-line captions.

## Text

- Preserve punctuation from the approved script.
- Preserve capitalization from the approved script.
- Do not invent full stops inside phrase chunks.
- Do not caption silent hooks, pauses, or silent CTA screens unless requested.

## Style

- Keep one line visible at a time.
- Use a backing box that supports readability without feeling like a lower-third banner.
- Preview on a visually busy frame before full burn-in.
- Reduce font size if captions overpower UI.
- Raise bottom margin if captions collide with product controls or chart labels.

## Proof Points

Spot-check:

- First narration line.
- Questions.
- Scene title reveal.
- Product demo phrase.
- Final narrated line.
